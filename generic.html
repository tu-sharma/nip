Problem

India is a huge Populus Country. This country has many services that started for the people of India and their benefits. For Process, the benefits in this huge country of 1.3 B people, Govt. of India introduced many identities on the basis of their needs to people and interests. For eg: Voter ID, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, etc. The problem is that millions of people registered for these IDs and have many issues with these identities like an update of new policies, prior details of IDs and main issues are the major change to maintain data of identities.

To get info about the notifications and identities, every citizen is always in stress because of inauthentic resources or lack of proper knowledge. Unnecessary, Amount charged by the citizen as a cause of Govt. Services charges that maybe doesn't exist. Customer Services are also very small to the huge people, therefore they can't manage the people's queries.

Our Solution

Our Idea is to respond to this problem by the National ID integration portal that helps every citizen to access the customer services of their relevant Identity queries. NIP Assistant is a bot that learns about ID's and the several details to help people. Users can ask the question to a bot like Queries about aadhaar. Future Scope: It will also help to access the directly details from Govt. official data and also provide other direct, indirect services.
